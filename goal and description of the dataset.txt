Goal:

To implement a spam classification process so that spam items could be reliably identified. The data was classified as either spam, or ham. For the purposes of this report, “ham” is defined as a text document that was useful to the user, and originating from a known or friendly source.

Dataset:

The dataset is comprised of 5,572 documents. Each document is assigned either the class of “spam” or “ham,” as noted above. Out of the total number of documents, 4,825, or 86.5%, were categorized as ham.
